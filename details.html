Permanent Residence - Eligibility

If you continuously reside in Korea for three years after acquiring a residency visa and meet the criteria of the 'points-based system,' where you earn points based on factors such as:

  • Educational qualifications
  • Research experience
  • Research results
  • Korean language proficiency
  • Residence in non-metropolitan areas

Bonus Points: If you currently live in a non-metropolitan area or a designated Depopulation Region, you will receive bonus points.

Permanent Residency Visa for High-Potential Talents in Science and Technology (F-5-S1)

Eligibility

  • F-2-7S and F-2-7* visa holders

Since February 10, 2023, F-2-7 visa holders have become eligible to take part in the fast-track program.

Requirements

  • Have resided continuously in Korea for at least three years.
  • Determined to be qualified through the points-based system (scored at least 90 out of a total of 200 points).

Note: You still need to meet the same Basic Requirements as when applying for an F-2-7S visa.
